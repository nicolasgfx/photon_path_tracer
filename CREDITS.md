## Conference Room

- **License:** Credit required (custom — see note below)
- **Copyright:** © Anat Grynberg and Greg Ward
- **Source:** https://casual-effects.com/data

Original description from the authors:
> "This model was created over a period of three weeks by painstaking
> measurements and many vi editing sessions. The real conference room looks
> very similar to the Radiance renderings as a consequence, and we would be
> interested to compare results to other rendering techniques if you have them.
> **Please do not use this model for any purpose without giving proper credit.**"

OBJ conversion and geometry cleanup by Kenzie Lamar (Vicarious Visions).
Original in RADIANCE format by Anat Grynberg and Greg Ward,
Lawrence Berkeley National Laboratory, circa 1991.
